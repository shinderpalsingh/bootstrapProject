Royal Enfield Opens New Store in Ireland

Iconic motorcycle manufacturer Royal Enfield has opened its first dealership in Ireland. The dealership is a partnership with Co Limerick-based RetroVentures. Royal Enfield already has a good image in Ireland, thanks to RetroVentures, which has been hiring out Royal Enfield motorcycles for over two years

Royal Enfield Takes 5th Position in Motorcycle Sales

Royal Enfield has climbed into the list of top 5 motorcycle manufacturers in India, on the back of stupendous growth. And in the process, Royal Enfield has overtaken Yamaha India, which now sits at 6th position. In the financial year 2015-16, Royal Enfield sold 4,98,791 units

Royal Enfield Opens Stores in Paris

Royal Enfield announced the opening of its first exclusive stores in the city of Madrid and Paris. The new stores in these countries is a part of Royal Enfield's growth strategy in Europe and focused international thrust of leading and expanding the global mid-sized motorcycle segment
